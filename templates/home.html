Fitness Tips

BMR(Basal Metabolic Rate) : This is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions.

TDEE(Total Daily Energy Expenditure) : This is the total number of calories you need to maintain your current weight, considering your activity level.

Maintain a balanced diet with a mix of proteins, carbs, and fats.

Include strength training and cardio in your workout routine.

Stay hydrated and get enough sleep.

Track your progress and adjust your routine as needed.
